Projects

InPocket - Money Manager

InPocket was my first app I ever developed independently.

What is it?

InPocket is designed to be your own personal money manager. Firstly uses of the app, will create accounts and initalize their balance. Users will continue to add transactions that reflect the current state of their budgets. Following adding transactions, they can view several charts and a spreadsheet of their transaction history. You can also view a demo-video by clicking the video icon.

Congressional App Challenge

InPocket represented LA-06 (Congressman Garrett Graves) in the 2021 Congressional App Challenge. It was truly a honor being selected to represent my district infront of the country with my app. I want to give a huge thank you to Congressman Graves and the committee for selecting me. You can check out coverage on my app by clicking on a couple of the icons.
